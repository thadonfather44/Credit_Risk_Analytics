Business Requirements

Problem Statement
A retail bank needs visibility into which customers are likely to 
default on their loans and what factors drive poor loan performance.

Stakeholders
Credit Risk Manager
Collections Team
Executive Leadership

Requirements

Data Requirements
Customer demographic and financial profile data
Full loan history including status and outstanding balance
Payment behaviour history per loan

Dashboard Requirements
Executive summary with portfolio level KPIs
Default rate analysis by credit score, age, income and employment
Geographic breakdown by South African province
High risk customer watchlist ranked by defaulted exposure

KPIs to Track
Total loans and total customers
Portfolio default rate
Average interest rate
Total outstanding exposure
Default exposure on at risk loans

Success Criteria
The dashboard enables the credit risk team to identify 
high risk customers before they default and take proactive 
action to reduce portfolio default rate.
